About Me

When I was young, I was driven by the unknown. I would take apart anything I could get my hands on to see how it worked at its core. I would take notes on what I thought each component was doing and then put it back together. This innate curiosity has not evaded me as childhood has; however, it has evolved into something new: code.

I previously worked at a small startup soap company, and it was an incredible experience. I had the opportunity to solve many problems, design creative solutions to production scalability, and leverage my communication skills to lead a team through the growing pains of a young company. As time passed, the problems became more scarce, and I became unfulfilled and unsatisfied in my path. Enter: the ultimate career path for a problem-solver, software development.

I began my coding journey a year ago when I decided it was time to make a shift into a career where my need to be constantly learning is satisfied. I decided to leave my leadership role in the pursuit of learning to code full time. Over the last year, I have refined my skills in JavaScript, React / Redux, Python, and PostgreSQL, along with many other frameworks and tools. Check out my skills below for a comprehensive list.

Check out my projects below to see all this in action!
